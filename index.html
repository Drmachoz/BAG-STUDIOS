Synopsis

Set in an abandoned city where criminals reign, equipment is scarce, and fans watch their favorite contestants face off against new up and coming fighters, most of which end up dead. You play as a character that ends up in this colosseum and has to fight through waves of enemies, occasionally facing fan favorite contestants as mini bosses to gain freedom. Who knows, maybe you'll become the next fan favorite?

Enemies

The player will face different enemies throughout the waves, each with their own behavior and abilities. Some enemies use shields and melee attacks, while others stay at range and force the player to keep moving and change their playstyle.

The Arena

The arena is where the player fights through waves of enemies, with short breaks between rounds. As the waves progress, new enemy types and minibosses appear, making each fight more challenging.

Combat

The game is a third-person shooter with both ranged and melee attacks. The player can freely aim and shoot enemies, use basic melee attacks, lock on for extra moves, and activate abilities during battle.
